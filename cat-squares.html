Cat Squares

Launch Project🚀

Cat Squares is photo scrambling puzzle game.

What is Cat Squares?

Cat Squares is the product of a little weekend javascript project that takes a square image and randomises it. It's then up to you to put it back in order by clicking the tile you want to move, followed by the tile you want to swap position with. It keeps track of how many tiles are currently in their correct location and gives you a score at the end of how many moves it took you to assemble.


Why Cat Squares?

Cat Squares is just an idea I had for a simple concept I could build in a weekend. Also I have a surplus of pictures of my cats so I might as well put them to good use.
