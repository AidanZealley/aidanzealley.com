Caroline Hudson

Launch Project🚀

Client project for a university module - logo, photography, website and custom CMS.

What did I do?

As part of a team project at university we were assigned to local artist Caroline Hudson who was in need of a website and branding. We were in competition against 7 other teams working for the same client, with only the best being chosen for real use. I was assigned to design and development.

What did the product do?

The website features a fully responsive front end with an ajax loading gallery, blog and events listings. A CMS was developed in PHP to manage these three areas.

We also set up Mailchimp with a custom template and did some studio photographs of her work to populate the gallery. The 'studio' consisted of a small roll of seamless paper and a speedlite+softbox which I took over to her gallery.
