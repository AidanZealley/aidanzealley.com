Responsive, single page site with touch gallery.

What is Ebor House

Ebor House is a Victorian holiday cottage in Brompton, North Yorkshire.

The site was designed to reflect the age of the building while maintaining a fresh, modern feel and without looking dated.

It features a full width touch gallery, PHP contact form and integrated google map in the footer.
